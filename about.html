About Chronika

**Chronika** is a project dedicated to preserving and presenting history in an engaging, interactive way. Our mission is to bring historical events to life with a detailed timeline, informative articles, and a visually appealing experience.

Our Mission

We aim to make history accessible to everyone, from casual enthusiasts to researchers, by curating accurate and well-researched historical narratives.
